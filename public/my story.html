My Story

I worked a lot since I turned 18. I would summarize that part of story
which took place before me become a web developer. I did a lot of
sales job in my life and actually I am good at it, but now when
it comes to sales again, I feel very bad about it.

When I was a child, I always dreamed about being a developer
who is sitting behind the computer and runs the whole world.
I would always loved being a software engineer but I don't know
what happened to me that I chose another engineering major and
more than that, to become a sales expert that doing it makes me sick now.

Eventually, I made up my mind and decided to be a developer
in order to enjoy my job for the rest of my life.
Now, that I learned about programming and web development,
believe it or not, I'm very happy. I wake up with motivation
in the morning and the whole day I have so much energy for doing
this kind of tasks.
